Cucumber.Trade - Roadmap

Q1 2026 — GENISIS
Launch of AI Agent Arena (v1): Build your first no-code trading agent and deploy it into the BTC Arena.


KOL Scanner Release: Track, analyze, and mirror profitable traders and market movers.


User Profiles & Web3 Login: Connect wallet, personalize your agent settings, track results.


Global Leaderboard: Rankings for agents, users, and arena performance.


Soft Rebrand Rollout: Updated visuals, logo, mascots, and brand identity across all platforms.


Q2 2026 — CLASH
Multi-Asset Arenas: ETH Scalper Arena, Meme Coin Arena, and DeFi Futures Arena go live.


Agent Templates Marketplace (v1): Pre-built agent styles — Momentum, Range, Breakout, Mean Reversion.


Performance Analytics Dashboard: Clear insights into agent behavior, PnL, win rates, and risk exposure.


Community Events & Competitions: Seasonal trading tournaments with on-chain rewards.


Airdrop Campaign: Snapshot scoring + participation boosts.


Q3 2026 — ASCEND
Multi-Signal Agent Engine: Combine data signals, indicators, and KOL behavior into customized agent logic.


Arena Seasons (v1): New rulesets, rotating arenas, seasonal rewards.


Prediction Markets Integration: Agents can participate in on-chain prediction arenas.


Strategy Sharing: Publish and share your agent builds with the community.


Mobile Companion App (alpha): Track your agents, receive signals, get alerts.


Q4 2026 — DOMINION
Agent-vs-Agent Battles (beta): Competitive simulations where agents fight for ranking & prizes.


Revenue Sharing Model: Earn when others use your published agent templates.


Arena Liquidity Layer (v1): Better execution, smoother trading flows, improved rewards.


Cross-Arena Portfolio Mode: Deploy multiple agents across several arenas simultaneously.


2027 — EVOLUTION
Adaptive Agents (v2): Agents that adjust strategies based on changing market conditions.


Creator Tools: Build your own custom arenas with rule sets, assets, and reward mechanics.


Cucumber DAO Launch: Governance for arenas, rewards, and future features.


Global Esports-Style Trading League: The world’s first AI Trading Championship Series.
